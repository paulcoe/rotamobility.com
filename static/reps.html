Interested in becoming a ROTA Representative?

As a ROTA Rep, you’ll host clinics, deliver demos, and build relationships with our customers. You’ll collaborate with the ROTA team and help run marketing activities and promotions to increase awareness of ROTA’s products. This is a position that takes energy, enthusiasm, and passion. It takes an understanding of people and the ability to spread the word through your community. It’s a chance to help people discover how they can better their health and well-being.
