Power is applied to the rear wheel with the greatest traction. Wheels rotate independently when cornering.

Powertrain

Integrated powertrain. Delivers power on push and pull from propulsion lever to both rear wheels.

Internal hub gear

Double chain powertrain with an internal hub gear. Change between 8 speeds with grip shift.

Foot boards

Easily accessible, non-slip, and room to adjust.

Steering halo

Integrated into propulsion lever. Connects steering linkage to front fork crown.

Row to go. Pull to brake.

To propel RoTrike push forward and pull back in a rowing motion on the font-and-center mounted handlebar. To steer just turn the handlebar the direction you wish to go. To brake just pull back all the way on the handlebar.

It’s all ergonomic, simple and intuitive.
