Chris Bayne, President & CEO, Co-Founder

Educated at the University of London in Material Science, Chris has over 30 years designing, building, and selling equipment for the semiconductor and medical Industries. Chris had a distinguished international twenty-one year career with Heraeus GmbH where he held executive positions in both Sales and Technical Marketing. He went on to found Diamond Semiconductor and Sales Management Innovations. Chris has been awarded a total of 7 US patents.

Michael Bayne, Operations, Co-Founder

Prior to co-founding ROTA, Michael was part of the engineering design team responsible for the first iPad at Apple. Previous to his work at Apple, Michael was a manufacturing engineer at VLSI Standards– a subsidiary of KLA-Tencor. Michael earned his M.B.A and Masters in Systems Engineering from San Jose State University where he was a GTI and Wang Foundation scholar.

Cindy Goodsell, Finance

Cindy earned a BS degree in finance from Brigham Young University and an MBA with an emphasis in information systems from Santa Clara University. Cindy brings 25 years of business and accounting experience to the team. Over the years, she has worked in a variety of industries and positions including as a systems analyst, financial analyst, controller, and staff accountant. For the past 10 years, she has owned her own practice – AccountAbility.

Amrik Atwal, Manufacturing

Amrik studied manufacturing at the Warwick School, managed metal fabrication operations at Computer Cabinet Corporation, and is now Founder and CEO of A&J Precision Sheetmetal. A&J provides product design, engineering, and manufacturing services for the electronics, semiconductor, and medical equipment industries.
